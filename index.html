Techno Cultural Club

With a motive to provide a common platform for Interdisciplinary technical activities of the college, Technical Club was launched in 2017. It aims to technically strengthen the students by integrating their skills in the various fields of Engineering & Technology, so as to cope up with the highly competitive environment.

Event

Our existence is all about Research and exploration. With this spirit Techno Cultural Club organised an event of research and explore. In this participents have to do the research about a particular field and present their research in the formet of ppt and have to explain about that field. In this the participents explore many field and puts their views. For this event our judges are Miss Sangeeta maam, Mr. Panchanan Payare Sir and Mr.Krishna Kumar Sir. This event was followed by award giving ceremony on our various events held earlier like research by learning hackathon and creators web design contest.
